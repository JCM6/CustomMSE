                  Keyboard shortcuts

                  Card commands

                  • hold click, spacebar: view card details.
                  • q, w, e: increase power, toughness or counters amount.
                  • Q, W, E: decrease power, toughness or counters amount.
                  • t: tap or untap.
                  • f: facedown or transform.
                  • a, b, y, Y, g, x: moveto Hand, Battlefield, top of Library, bottom of Library, Graveyard or Exiled.

                  Global commands

                  • d: draw a card.
                  • alt+q, alt+w, alt+g: increase power, toughness or counters amount on all cards.
                  • alt+Q, alt+W, alt+G: decrease power, toughness or counters amount on all cards.
                  • l, L: increase or decrease life counter.
                  • p, P: increase or decrease poison counter.
                  • n: next turn.
                  • u: untap all cards.
                  • h: hide hand.
                  • r: roll dice.
                  • s: shuffle.
                  • c: put commander in command zone.
                  • m: mulligan.
                  • R: restart.
                  • S: show the settings dialog.
                  • H: show this dialog.
